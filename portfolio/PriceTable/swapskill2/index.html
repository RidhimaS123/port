FAQ

How to create a profile?

Creating a profile is easy! Just add your skills, interests, and learning goals along with your email ID.

How does skill swapping work?

List the skills you want to share and the ones you want to learn. Our algorithm will find your perfect match!

Can I chat with potential partners?

Yes, you can chat via text or video with people whose profiles interest you.

How accurate are listed skills?

We verify skills by testing users to ensure they are genuine and accurate.

Is the matching algorithm reliable?

Our algorithm considers skill level, location, and preferences to provide accurate matches.
